Erika Novak & Drew Darley

We are a team of two ceramic artists and partners

Every piece of pottery is hand thrown on a potter's wheel and carved or finished with extra attention to detail.

Read Our Story

Our founding

The two began making ceramics in 2015 at Central Connecticut State University. Both Erika and Drew were pursuing their undergrads in different disciplines; Drew was studying for a math degree and Erika was working toward an environmental geography degree. They met when taking a ceramics elective class at the university. After graduation they opened a ceramics studio together, where they spent the next few years finding their artistic voices and refining their distinctive styles.

We also like to play

Working with clay has not only been our passion but it has become a lifestyle. We live our lives completely dedicated to our craft. Making, building, creating, and selling our artworks and building our business is everything to us. We also love that this lifestyle allows us the freedom to play and explore! If we are not in the studio you can find us traveling around in our van, getting lost, Hiking,climbing rocks, or catching fish. We recently completed a thur hike of the Appalachian Trail walking non-stop from Georgia to Maine.
